BriBot Music Player Update patch notes:
- BriBot will now play music in the gaming channel when commanded
- play command, shuffles music from God vQ (spotify playlist)
- stop command, stops the music session and BriBot leaves the channel
- pause command, pauses the current song
- resume command, resumes the song after pausing (does not work after stop)
- volume <number> command, adjusts the volume, the number is a multiplier
- play song <song name> command, plays the first search result on Youtube for entered <song name>
- play link <link> command, plays the audio from a youtube link
- commands can be sent in any channel or direct messages to BriBot
